With over a decade of development experience in a number of industries I realized that the typical product creation process between clients and developers was flawed. For this reason I decided to utilize a completely different development pattern that focuses on highly focused communicaion and transparent design patterns. when I take on a project not only do I work to craft he application according to he client's specifications, i give daily updates to ensure that the project is aligning properly with the client's goals. The end result is the right product, delivered on time, and on budget.
